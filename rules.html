Server Rules

We try to keep as few rules as possible, but every minecrat server needs them to avoid becoming a hellhole or violating the eula

Boring List:

  • No hacked clients, unfair advantages, cheat mods or anything in that category (bannable, first offence: 1w, second offence: perm)
  • No directing discrinination to any group, religion, sex, race or any other factor (mutable&bannable, first offence: mute[12h], second offence: perm ban)
  • No excessive cursing. (bannable&mutable, first offence: 1h mute, second offence: 1w ban, third offence: perm ban)
  • No advertising other servers (bannable&mutable, first offence: 1d ban+3d mute, second offence: perm ban)
  • Do not at all attempt to crash, lag, or otherwise harm members of the community or the server (bannable, first offence: 1d, second offence: perm)
  • Do not talk about (or actually) ddos/dox any member of the community (bannable, forst offence: perm)
  • That's all, and please ffs, USE COMMON SENSE!
